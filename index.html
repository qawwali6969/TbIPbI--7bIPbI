Core Workflow

  • Merchant authorizes a Pinterest account through OAuth.
  • The tool reads selected board data for destination choice.
  • The tool publishes original product Pins from approved listings.

Content Policy

Kinoshara Pinterest Publisher is intended for original merchant content only. It is not designed for scraping, competitor monitoring, or republishing third-party Pins.

Support

Authorized Use

The integration is used only with an authorized Pinterest account connected by the merchant.

Original Assets

Published Pins are based on approved Kinoshara listings, original product imagery, and brand-owned creative assets.
