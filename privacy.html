Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Kinoshara collects, uses, and protects information when a visitor uses the Kinoshara website, contacts the brand, places orders through linked sales channels, or authorizes the Kinoshara Pinterest integration.

Last updated: March 7, 2026

1. Who We Are

Kinoshara is a small brand and online seller offering curated products through its website presence and linked marketplace listings.

This website serves as Kinoshara's public information hub and also supports the Pinterest integration used for authorized account connections, board selection, and original product Pin publishing workflows.

Contact for privacy requests: privacy@kinoshara.app.

2. Information We Receive

Depending on how a visitor interacts with Kinoshara, we may receive:

  • Contact details and message contents when someone reaches out to us for support, order questions, or privacy requests.
  • Basic order-related information that may be shared through the marketplace or sales platform where a customer places an order.
  • Pinterest account identifiers and profile information necessary to recognize the authorized user.
  • Board and Pin data required to read selected boards, review authorized content destinations, or create original product Pins on the user's behalf.
  • Technical data from this website, such as browser type, referral URL, IP address, and timestamped access logs.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only for the purposes necessary to operate the Kinoshara website, support customers, and run the Pinterest integration when a user chooses to authorize it.

  • To respond to customer questions about listings, orders, returns, or support requests.
  • To complete Pinterest OAuth authorization and verify that a user granted access.
  • To call Pinterest API endpoints requested by the user, such as reading selected board data or publishing original product Pins from approved Kinoshara listings.
  • To troubleshoot errors, prevent abuse, and maintain service integrity.
  • To respond to support inquiries, legal requests, and data deletion requests.
We do not ask for or store a user's Pinterest password. Authentication is handled by Pinterest on Pinterest-owned pages.

4. Pinterest API Permissions

The Kinoshara app may request a subset of the following Pinterest scopes, depending on the merchant workflow being used:

  • `boards:read` to view board metadata needed for destination selection.
  • `boards:write` to create or update boards when a user explicitly requests it.
  • `pins:read` to retrieve Pin data associated with the authorized account when needed for workflow review.
  • `pins:write` to publish original product Pins on behalf of the authorized user.

We request only the permissions necessary for the intended Pinterest feature.

5. OAuth and Token Handling

  1. The user is sent to Pinterest to log in and approve access.
  2. Pinterest redirects the user to the registered Kinoshara callback URL with an authorization code.
  3. That code is exchanged for an access token by Kinoshara infrastructure using Pinterest's OAuth token endpoint.
  4. The token is used only for the authorized Pinterest actions and may be revoked by the user through Pinterest settings.
If a public demo page displays the returned authorization code for review purposes, that display alone does not mean the code is stored permanently on the static site.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only in the following limited situations:

  • With Pinterest, as necessary to complete the OAuth flow and API requests initiated by the user.
  • With hosting, security, or logging providers that help operate the website and only under appropriate confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide customer support, maintain the website, operate the Pinterest integration, document consent, resolve disputes, comply with law, or prevent abuse.

Users may request deletion of stored data by contacting privacy@kinoshara.app.

8. User Choices

  • You may decline to authorize the Pinterest app.
  • You may revoke Kinoshara's access at any time from your Pinterest account settings.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of data we hold about you, subject to applicable law.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information processed. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we limit access and use HTTPS for public web access.

10. Children's Privacy

The Kinoshara Pinterest integration is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted on this page with the effective date shown above.
